# Ribeiro et al. (2021) — Beyond Accuracy: Behavioral Testing of NLP Models with CheckList

- **DOI:** https://doi.org/10.24963/ijcai.2021/659
- **Authors:** Marco Tulio Ribeiro, Tongshuang Wu, Carlos Guestrin, Sameer Singh
- **Year:** 2021
- **Source:** CrossRef

## Key Findings

CheckList provides a task-agnostic behavioral testing methodology and shows that high-performing NLP models still have critical, pattern-specific bugs.

## Relevance to This Project

Methodological backbone for ABSA-RTS: category-driven diagnostics, failure tracking, and reliability-oriented evaluation beyond macro-F1.
